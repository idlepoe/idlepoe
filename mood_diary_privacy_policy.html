Mood Diary does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your mood data remains completely private and is stored only on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Mood Diary is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Mood data or emotional records
  • Usage analytics or app interaction data
  • Device information or unique identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

2. Data Storage

All your mood diary entries and app settings are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No cloud servers or external databases are used
  • Your information cannot be accessed by third parties
  • You have complete control over your data

3. Permissions

Mood Diary may request the following permissions for app functionality:

  • Notification Permission: To send daily mood reminder notifications (optional)
  • Storage Permission: To save your mood data locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

Mood Diary does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates independently without external data processing.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security risks related to external storage or transmission. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security measures.

6. Children's Privacy

Mood Diary does not collect any information from users of any age. The app is safe for users of all ages as no personal data is processed.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Since we do not collect personal information, changes to this policy will not affect your privacy.

8. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your local data through the app's settings.
